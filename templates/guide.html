This is a guide for downloading the takeout data from the google data dashboard

Please follow the steps written below to download your takeout data from the google data dashboard here :

  1. Visit the Google dashboard (https://myaccount.google.com/dashboard) and login to your google account
  2. Choose the "Download your data" button in the centre-left of the page. This will redirect you to a new page
  3. Go to the "Select data to include" checkbox section
  4. deselect every option selected by default in the 48 item list
  5. select the checkbox for the google pay data
  6. click on the "next step" button
  7. Choose the "Choose file type, frequency & destination" section below the data selection section
  8. In the "transfer to" drop-down list, choose the "send download link via email" option
  9. In the frequency section, select the "export once" option
  10. In the "file type and size" section, select the file type as .zip and the file size as "1GB"
  11. click on the "create export" button to generate the download url
  12. Go to the mail sent to you by the google data dashboard in your personal email
  13. Select the "Download your files" option
  14. In the "Manage you exports" section, click on the download button, this would download the zipped data on your personal data
  15. Open the google drive page, click on the "sign-in" option in the top right section of the page, and select the account to log in from
  16. This will open up the "your drive" page; click on the "new" button in the top left section of the page, and select the "file upload" option to select the zip file from your local computer
  17. After uploading the file, browse the "my drive" section, right-click on the add button, and select the "get link" option
  18. In the "general access" section, change the visibility from restricted to "anyone with the link" and then change the write access of the file to "editor"
  19. Click on the "Copy Link" button at the bottom left of the dialog box, the link is now copied on your clipboard
  20. Paste this link into the form
